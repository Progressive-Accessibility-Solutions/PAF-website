# Progressive Accessibility Foundation (PAF)

## About Us
Progressive Accessibility Foundation (PAF) is dedicated to building an inclusive digital world by ensuring that technology is accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities.

We work at the intersection of accessibility, technology, and education to promote equitable access to digital experiences.

---

## Mission
To empower organizations, developers, and communities to create accessible and inclusive digital products that work for all users.

---

## What We Do

- Accessibility Audits  
  Evaluate websites, applications, and digital products against global accessibility standards.

- Consulting & Strategy  
  Provide expert guidance on integrating accessibility into product lifecycles.

- Training & Workshops  
  Conduct hands-on sessions on accessibility best practices, ARIA, and inclusive design.

- Standards & Advocacy  
  Contribute to global accessibility initiatives and promote adoption of standards.

---

## Key Focus Areas

- Web Accessibility (WCAG)  
- ARIA Authoring Practices  
- Inclusive UX Design  
- Assistive Technology Support  
- Accessible Documentation and EPUB
