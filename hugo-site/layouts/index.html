Our Process

From inaccessible to
screen-reader perfect

A rigorous multi-stage conversion process with quality checks at every step.

1

Submit Content

Share physical books, scanned PDFs, or digital files. Assessment within 48 hours.

2

Intake & Analysis

We categorise content types, identify complex elements, and assign specialist handlers.

3

Expert Conversion

Specialists process text, math, visuals, and structure with detailed alt-text treatment.

4

Dual QA Review

Reviewed by a sighted accessibility expert and a blind quality reviewer using assistive tech.

5

Certified Delivery

Files delivered in EPUB3, DAISY, BRF, or Tagged PDF with a compliance certificate.

Research Wing

Advancing the science of
accessible learning

We study how blind learners comprehend complex information and publish findings that improve standards across the field.

Diagram Comprehension Research

Studying how tactile and audio-described diagrams are processed by blind learners — from simple bar charts to complex molecular structures.

Flowchart Accessibility Studies

Developing methods for conveying logical sequences and decision trees to learners using screen readers and refreshable Braille displays.

STEM Equation Readability

Comparing MathML rendering, LaTeX-to-speech, and manual narration for mathematical and scientific equations in educational contexts.

Regional Language Accessibility

Pioneering work on Hindi and other Indian language STEM textbooks — addressing script complexity and technical vocabulary challenges.

Collaborate on Research

We welcome partnerships with universities, IITs, IIMs, and international research institutions to co-author papers and share datasets.
